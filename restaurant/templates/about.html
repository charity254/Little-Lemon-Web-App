Our Story

A tale of two brothers, a passion for Mediterranean cuisine, and a dream brought to life in Nairobi

From Italy to Nairobi


Little Lemon was founded in 2015 by brothers Mario and Adrian Rossi, who grew up in a small coastal town in southern Italy. From a young age, they spent their evenings watching their grandmother fill the kitchen with the aromas of fresh basil, slow-cooked tomatoes, and grilled fish straight from the Mediterranean Sea. Those memories never left them.

Mario went on to train as a professional chef in Rome and Florence, mastering the art of Italian and broader Mediterranean cooking. Adrian, the business mind of the two, studied hospitality management and developed a deep passion for creating dining experiences that felt warm, personal, and memorable. Together, they were a perfect team.

After years of working in restaurants across Europe, the brothers felt a pull toward something bigger — a place where their food could truly stand out. A chance trip to Nairobi in 2013 changed everything. They were struck by the city's energy, its growing appetite for world-class cuisine, and the warmth of its people. They knew instantly — this was where Little Lemon belonged.

In 2015, they opened their doors in the vibrant Westlands neighborhood of Nairobi. The response was overwhelming. Nairobi embraced Little Lemon not just as a restaurant, but as an experience — a little corner of the Mediterranean in the heart of East Africa.

Today, Little Lemon remains true to its roots. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the same passion and attention to detail that their grandmother instilled in them. We source locally from Kenyan farms whenever possible, import specialty ingredients directly from the Mediterranean, and prepare everything fresh every single day. No shortcuts. Just love on a plate.

"Our mission is simple: bring the authentic taste of the Mediterranean to Nairobi, one plate at a time."

— Mario & Adrian Rossi
